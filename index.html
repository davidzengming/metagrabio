Metagrab

A blazing-fast platform
built for all gaming needs.

Metagrab is a gaming social platform app built for users to discover new games, find upcoming major releases, and chat with other users on the platform. From discovering games to jumping into discussions is only a few taps away, joining a community has never been this easy.

A place with plenty of features and room to talk

All games at a glance. Discussions and game details within a tap. Metagrab was built with both rich features and ease of navigation in mind.

Dynamic Media Viewer

Uploading and viewing media is incredibly simple. Zoom in to see all the details or zoom out where Metagrab dynamically scales them to make most of your screen.

Major Releases Timeline

Wondering what is coming up in a few weeks? You can easily take a look in the timeline, it's so fast and intuitive.

Vibrant Threads View

Chat via text, images, or emojis. The choice is yours.

Beautiful Dark Mode

In addition to the gorgeous light-mode, Metagrab is also optimized for the dark-mode crowd. Enjoy reading content using the tweaked contrast dark-mode.
